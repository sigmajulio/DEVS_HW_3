Sakura Kinomoto - The Cardcaptor

Age: 10 years old

Birthday: April 1st

About: Sakura is a cheerful elementary school student who accidentally releases the magical Clow Cards. She becomes the Cardcaptor and must capture all the cards to prevent disaster. She's energetic, kind, and loves sports and her friends.

Magic: Uses the Sealing Wand to capture Clow Cards and later transforms them into Sakura Cards.

Syaoran Li - The Descendant

Age: 10 years old

Birthday: July 13th

About: Syaoran comes from Hong Kong as a descendant of Clow Reed. Initially Sakura's rival for the Clow Cards, he eventually becomes her friend and ally. He's serious, skilled in martial arts, and uses Chinese magic.

Magic: Uses elemental magic and sword techniques. Can sense magical auras.

Tomoyo Daidouji - The Best Friend

Age: 10 years old

Birthday: September 3rd

About: Tomoyo is Sakura's best friend and biggest supporter. She has no magical powers but helps Sakura by designing beautiful costumes for her adventures and filming everything with her video camera.

Talents: Excellent at sewing, singing, and filming. Very intelligent and caring.

Cerberus (Kero-chan) - The Guardian

Age: Very old!

Form: Small stuffed animal (sealed) / Winged lion (true form)

About: Kero is the Guardian of the Clow Cards created by Clow Reed. He guides Sakura in her role as Cardcaptor. He looks like a cute stuffed animal but is actually a powerful magical beast.

Likes: Sweets, video games, and taking naps. Gets grumpy when hungry!
